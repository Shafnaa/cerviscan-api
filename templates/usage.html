About CerviScan

CerviScan is an innovative application designed for the early detection of cervical cancer using Visual Inspection with Acetic Acid (VIA) images. This detection is powered by a machine learning model, ensuring accurate and efficient results.

Usage Instructions

1. Go to the Go Scan page.

2. Fill out the Profile Card by entering the patient's first name, last name, and date of birth.

3. Upload a VIA image. Ensure the image is cropped to focus on the region of interest before uploading.

4. Press the Go Scan button below the upload field to start the prediction process.

5. View the prediction results and details on the Result Card.

6. To save the results to the album, press Save. Press Retake Scan to redo the detection.
